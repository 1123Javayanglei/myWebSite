  • The records and personal bests continue for the swimming and diving teams and the Brewers are making a splash nationally. In 2019, first year Jesse Ecklund swam three events at the NCAA nationals and earned All-America honors with a 4th place finish in the 200m backstroke. Ecklund and Max White were multiple winners at the Liberty League championships and diver Anthony Tobin qualified for the NCAA regionals.
